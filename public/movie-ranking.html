The Best Movies According to Nikhil

My top 3 movies of all time

3 Idiots

This movie is about three students who joined a pioneer engineering college of India and their journey of struggle through daily lives, competetiveness and finding themselves through it.

The Avengers: Endgame

A finest piece of storyline concluding with all the different superheroes of MCU come together to save Earth from Thanos.

The Dark Knight Rises

The final movie of Dark Night triology and final movie of Christian Bale as Batman.
The movie shows how after having everything in life now the batman has nothing and is down broken in a prison underground. It shows the journey of Batman from learning how to walk to defeating the most dangerous enemy he ever faced.
